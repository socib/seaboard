SOCIB Seaboard

This application shows real time data and forecasts from different locations at Balearic Islands coast. It is available in the hall of some hotels which collaborate with the SOCIB Beach Monitoring Programme, with the aim to inform that the hotel contributes to Coastal Science advance and to the beach and environment conservation, giving some usefull information at the same time.

SOCIB, the Balearic Islands Coastal Observing and Forecasting System, is an initiative to deep in linking socio-economic interests and environmental sustainability based on interdisciplinary and integral scientific knowledge.
